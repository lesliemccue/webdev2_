Overview of Social Media

Social media is a way of life nowadays. People from all walks of life use social media for various reasons, whether it’s too communicate with people, promote business’s or even to have a voice in various worldwide issues they would not normally be able to contribute to without the use of social media. Social media, however, is now an excellent tool for education. This website is here for you to explore different social media apps and read about how they can be used for teaching, the cautions and also how to install and set up the app if you choose to do so.
